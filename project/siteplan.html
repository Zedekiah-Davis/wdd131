Custom Keyboards Project Site Plan

Site Name: Custom Keyboards Hub

Reason: This site serves as a resource for enthusiasts and hobbyists interested in custom keyboard builds and components.

Site Purpose:

The site provides detailed guides, resources, and community insights on building and customizing keyboards, from beginner-friendly tutorials to advanced modifications.

Scenarios:

  • What is a custom keyboard, and why build one?
  • How do I start building my first custom keyboard?
  • What are the best switches, keycaps, and cases available?
  • Who should I contact if I have questions about specific builds or components?

  • Background: Soft Violet #D8BFD8
